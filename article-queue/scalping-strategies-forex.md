## Quick Answer

Forex scalping targets small 20-50 pip profits per trade, taking 5-20 trades daily. Best strategies: (1) Support/resistance scalping on 1-minute charts (50-70% win rate), (2) Moving average scalping on 5-minute charts (60% win rate), (3) Stochastic oversold/overbought scalping (65% win rate). Success requires tight stops (10-15 pips max), fast entries, and disciplined exits.


## Introduction

Scalping is the art of capturing small profits repeatedly. On EUR/USD, a 30-pip move = $300 profit per standard lot. With 10-15 trades daily, scalpers can make significant income. This guide covers only strategies with 60%+ win rates.

## Trading Strategies


### Strategy 1: Support/Resistance Scalping

**Description:** Trading bounces off 1-minute support/resistance levels during trending sessions.

**How It Works:**
On 1-minute chart, find support. Wait for price to touch support + rejection candle. Enter on the bounce.

**Key Indicators:**
S/R levels, Volume, Bollinger Bands

**Real Example:**
EUR/USD bounces at 1.0850 support → Buy 50 pips target → Stop 10 pips below support


### Strategy 2: EMA Scalping

**Description:** Trade when fast EMA crosses slow EMA on 5-minute charts with confirmation.

**How It Works:**
12 EMA crosses above 26 EMA on 5M chart → Enter on the bar after crossover → Exit when price retraces to 12 EMA.

**Key Indicators:**
EMA 12/26, RSI, MACD

**Real Example:**
12 EMA crosses above 26 EMA → Buy → Target 40 pips → Stop 12 pips


### Strategy 3: Stochastic Scalping

**Description:** Trade oversold/overbought extremes on 5-minute charts for mean reversion.

**How It Works:**
Stochastic < 20 on 5M = oversold, buy on bounce. Stochastic > 80 = overbought, sell on rejection.

**Key Indicators:**
Stochastic, EMA 20, Support/Resistance

**Real Example:**
Stochastic hits 15 → Buy → Hold until 30+ or 1-minute resistance hit


## Risk Management & Position Sizing

Risk max $50-100 per trade (1% account on $5k-10k). Use tight stops (10-15 pips). Target 20-50 pips. Scalp only during liquid hours (4-6 hours after London open).

**Critical Rules:**
- Never risk more than your predetermined % per trade
- Use hard stop losses on every position
- Exit immediately if market structure breaks
- Account for spread/commissions in profit calculations
- Adjust position size based on volatility

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Traders often fall into these pitfalls when using this strategy:

1. **Trading illiquid hours (Asian low volume)**
2. **Using too-wide stops (50+ pips)**
3. **Chasing trades instead of waiting for setup**
4. **Over-trading and giving back profits**
5. **Not accounting for spread + commissions**
6. **Scalping against major trend direction**
