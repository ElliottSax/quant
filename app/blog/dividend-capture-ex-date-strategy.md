## Dividend Capture

**Dividend capture strategies** buy stocks before ex-dates to collect dividends, selling shortly after. While 3-5% dividend yields seem attractive, bid-ask spreads often eliminate gains (institutional strategy), making tax-loss harvesting more valuable for retail traders on holdings 60+ days. Returns: break-even to 20% annualized minus trading costs.

---

*Key Metrics: 0-20% annual return (net) | Tax savings: 20-30% | 1-3 days hold (but 60+ days for qualified status) | Cost focus: bid-ask spreads*
