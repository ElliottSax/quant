## Protective Put: Insurance for Stock

Own stock, buy OTM put = insurance policy—costs $100-200 for $5,000 stock = 2-4% insurance. Cap losses at $5,100, profit unlimited. Use when expecting earnings/macro risk (saves 10-15% crash losses)
