## Synthetic Long Positions: Reduce Margin Requirements with Call Spreads

Synthetic long positions using call spreads reduce margin requirements by 40-60% compared to stock ownership while maintaining delta-equivalent upside—delivering $50K capital efficiency on $100K positions through long call + short put synthetics, though limits maximum profit and adds time decay costs requiring careful strike selection 15-45 DTE.
