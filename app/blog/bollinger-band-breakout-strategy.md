## Bollinger Band Breakout Strategy

A **Bollinger Band breakout strategy** trades volatility expansions when price breaks outside the statistical bands, typically generating 40-60% annualized returns with 15-25% maximum drawdown. Breakouts signal regime changes, mean-reversion setups, or trend accelerations depending on position and timeframe.
