## Sector Rotation Business Cycle

**Sector rotation strategies** systematically shift from defensive stocks (utilities, staples) in downturns to cyclicals (tech, energy, financials) in upturns, capturing 40-70% outperformance annually. Requires business cycle analysis (ISM manufacturing, jobless claims, yield curve) for timing signals. Professional implementation yields 50-60% annual returns with 20-30% drawdown correlation to markets.
