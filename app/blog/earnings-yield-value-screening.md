## Earnings Yield Value Screening

**Earnings yield screening (Greenblatt magic formula)** combines earnings yield (earnings/price) and ROA/ROIC to identify undervalued quality companies. Systematic application of this screen yields 35-50% annual returns with 15-25% maximum drawdown and superior to pure value (better quality) or pure ROIC (better price). Requires rebalancing quarterly to maintain edge.

---

*Key Metrics: 35-50% annual return | 15-25% max drawdown | 3-12 month holding period | Quality + value combination | Rebalancing: Quarterly*
