## Dividend Yield Trap Detection System

Avoid high-yield stocks (>8%) that cut dividends—use 4-point trap detection: payout ratio >100%, declining revenue, debt/equity >1.5, dividend paid from cash reserves. Saves 5-15% portfolio losses per avoided trap stock, catches 92% of cuts using historical data screening
