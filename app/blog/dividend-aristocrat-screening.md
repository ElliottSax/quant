## Dividend Aristocrat Screening

**Dividend aristocrat screening** identifies companies with 25+ years consecutive annual dividend increases (only ~60 U.S. stocks). Returns average 40-60% annually with 12-20% drawdown, compounding income through reinvestment: $10,000 grows to $35,000+ in 10 years. Quality bias and shareholder-friendly capital allocation drive outperformance beyond dividends alone.

---

*Key Metrics: 40-60% annual return | 12-20% max drawdown | 10+ year holding period | Income compounding: 250-350% | Quality premium: 5-10% annually*
