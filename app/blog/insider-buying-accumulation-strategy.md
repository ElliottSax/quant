## Insider Buying Accumulation

**Insider buying strategy** follows officer/director accumulations in open market purchases (ignoring options/compensation), signaling conviction. Portfolios of high insider buy stocks deliver 50-70% annual returns with 15-25% drawdown, 12-24 month holding periods; institutional purchasing power increases probability.

*Key Metrics: 50-70% annual return | 15-25% max drawdown | 12-24 months hold | 65-75% win rate | $20,000+ capital recommended*
