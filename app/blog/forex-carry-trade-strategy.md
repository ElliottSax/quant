## Forex Carry Trade Strategy

**Carry trade strategies** profit from interest rate differentials between currency pairs (sell low-rate currencies, buy high-rate currencies). Returns average 60-100% annualized from interest earnings plus appreciation, with 25-40% drawdown risk during crisis depeggings, requiring 6-12 month minimum holding periods.

---

*Key Metrics: 60-100% annual return | 25-40% max drawdown | 6-12 months hold | 55-65% win rate | $25,000+ forex margin minimum*
