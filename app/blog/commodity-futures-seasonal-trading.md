## Commodity Futures Seasonal Trading

**Commodity seasonal strategies** exploit predictable supply-demand cycles: wheat (harvest lows), crude oil (winter demand), corn (planting season volatility). Seasonal patterns deliver 50-70% annual returns with 20-30% drawdown and 8-16 week holding periods aligned with production calendars.
