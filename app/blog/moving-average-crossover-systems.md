## Moving Average Crossover Systems

**Moving average crossover strategies** generate buy/sell signals when fast and slow averages cross, capturing full trends with minimal curve-fitting. Returns average 40-60% annually with 15-25% max drawdown and 4-12 week holding periods, with 60-70% win rates.
