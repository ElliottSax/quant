## Swing Trade Exit Management

Exit 50% at 1.5:1 RRR, 25% at 2.5:1 RRR, let final 25% run to resistance = locks in profit, stays long for big moves. Real example: $5,000 position risking $250 = $375 (50% profit) + $625 (25% profit) + trail stops = total $1,200-1,800 profit
