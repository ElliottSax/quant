## Low Debt High FCF Screening

**Low debt + high FCF screening** identifies financial fortress companies: debt/equity <0.5, FCF/net income >1.0. These companies outperform 50-70% annually with 10-18% drawdown through dividend growth, buybacks, and M&A optionality. Win rate: 70-80% by eliminating bankruptcy risk and capturing conservative management practices that compound returns.
