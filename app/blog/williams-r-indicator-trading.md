## Williams %R Indicator Trading

**Williams %R trading** identifies extreme momentum when indicator reaches -100 (oversold) or 0 (overbought), signaling mean reversion opportunities. Returns average 35-50% annually with 12-18% max drawdown and 2-5 week holding periods, with 65-75% win rates capturing bounces from extremes.
