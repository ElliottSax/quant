## Swing Trading Setup Requirements

Minimum account: $25K (PDT rule), $3K margin maintenance—trade 2-5 positions/week, hold 2-5 days, 2-5% risk per trade = $250-625/trade on $25K = $500-1,250/week at 2:1 RRR (2 wins/week avg)
