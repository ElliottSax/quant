## Merger Arbitrage

**Merger arbitrage** captures the spread between announced deal prices and current market prices, earning 15-30% returns over 2-12 month holding periods. Low correlation to markets (10-15% drawdown typical), but requires deep deal analysis and regulatory risk assessment for consistent execution.

---

*Key Metrics: 15-30% per deal return | 10-15% max drawdown | 2-12 months hold | 60-70% deal completion rate | $15,000+ required*
