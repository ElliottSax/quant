## Options Calendar Spread

**Calendar spread strategies** sell near-term options while buying longer-dated ones, profiting from theta decay (time value erosion). Returns range 25-40% monthly on margin with 15-25% max loss, ideal for sideways markets; 2-4 week rolling holding periods generate consistent monthly income.

---

*Key Metrics: 25-40% monthly return | 15-25% max loss | 2-4 weeks hold | 70-75% win rate | $10,000+ options margin required*
