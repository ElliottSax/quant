## Technical Support Accumulation

**Support accumulation strategy** enters bulk orders at 200+ day moving averages and long-term support levels, capturing 20-40% rebounds over 4-12 weeks. Requires volume confirmation (buyers showing up at support) and trend context (overall uptrend preference). Win rate: 60-70%, with 10-20% maximum loss if support breaks. Ideal for building positions in volatile stocks.
