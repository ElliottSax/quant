## MACD Divergence Trading

**MACD divergence trading** identifies price-momentum mismatches where price makes new highs/lows but MACD doesn't, signaling trend exhaustion. Strategy generates 45-65% annualized returns with 10-20% maximum drawdown over 2-8 week holding periods.
