## Market Breadth Analysis: Predicting Major Reversals and Breakouts

Market breadth (advance/decline ratios, cumulative breadth) predicts reversals 5-10 trading days before major moves by revealing hidden distribution/accumulation when S&P 500 hits new highs but breadth diverges negative—with 70%+ accuracy for identifying failed breakouts and 60%+ for confirming genuine reversals when combined with volume extremes.
