## CAPE Ratio Market Valuation

**CAPE ratio analysis** evaluates market-wide valuation using 10-year earnings averages, revealing over/undervaluation with 70%+ accuracy for 10-year returns. Current CAPE >30 suggests 0-5% returns next 10 years; CAPE <15 suggests 10-15% returns. Useful for portfolio allocation (cash/bonds/stocks) and sector rotation rather than tactical trading.

---

*Key Metrics: 70% accuracy for 10-year predictions | Current valuation signals long-term allocation | 0-5% return potential (high CAPE) | 10-15% return potential (low CAPE)*
