## Undervalued Cyclical Timing

**Cyclical value strategy** purchases out-of-favor cyclical stocks (energy, financials, materials) at <10x earnings after 40%+ declines. Captures 60-100% rebounds over 8-16 months as cycles recover; maximum risk: secular declines (energy/coal). 50-60% win rate, 40-50% annual returns when cycle turns.

*Key Metrics: 60-100% cyclical rebound | 8-16 months hold | 50-60% win rate | 40-50% annual return | Cycle timing critical | $20,000+ required*
