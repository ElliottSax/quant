## Stochastic Oversold Trading

**Stochastic oversold trading** enters when the fast stochastic drops below 20, signaling extreme momentum exhaustion and bounce-back potential. Returns average 40-55% annually with 12-18% max drawdown and 1-4 week holding periods capturing quick reversal moves.

---

*Key Metrics: 40-55% annual return | 12-18% max drawdown | 1-4 weeks hold | 65-75% win rate | $5,000+ minimum capital*
