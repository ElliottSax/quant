## Sentiment Analysis Trading: Forecasting Market Reversals Through Psychological Data

Sentiment analysis of news, social media, and earnings call transcripts predicts market reversals with 62-68% accuracy when extreme negative/positive readings diverge from price moves—generating 8-15% capture on reversals within 2-10 trading days by identifying capitulation (>95th percentile negative) and euphoria (>95th percentile positive) moments when price extremes coincide with sentiment inversions.

### Key Metrics

- Win Rate: 62-68% (sentiment divergence trades)
- Average Move Capture: 8-15% (reversal trades)
- Holding Period: 2-10 trading days
- Signal Reliability: 75-85% (extreme sentiment only)
- Data Sources: News, social media, transcripts

### Implementation Approach

Combining multiple sentiment sources (news wires, social media volume, options sentiment) improves signal reliability while reducing false positives from single-source analysis.
