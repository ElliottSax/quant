## RSI Oversold Bounce

An **RSI oversold bounce strategy** enters long positions when RSI falls below 30, targeting mean reversion trades with 3-6 month holding periods. Returns average 35-50% annually with 12-18% max drawdown, capturing strong bounce-backs in downtrends.

*Key Metrics: 35-50% annual return | 12-18% max drawdown | 3-6 weeks average hold | 60-70% win rate | $5,000+ minimum capital*
