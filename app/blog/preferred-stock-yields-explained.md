## Preferred Stock Dividend Yields Explained

Preferred stocks deliver 5-7% yields (senior to common dividends in bankruptcy)—typical $100 par preferred = $5-7 annual income per share. Tax-advantaged, lower volatility than bonds. Build 30% preferred/$70 dividend stock blended portfolio
