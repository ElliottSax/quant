## Relative Strength Sector Leader

**Relative strength leader strategy** buys stocks outperforming their sector and market by 10%+ over 3-6 months, riding momentum. Captures 35-55% gains over 6-12 months with 15-25% max drawdown. Win rate: 65-75% by identifying quality companies gaining share (margin expansion) rather than momentum-only stocks. Technical breakouts confirm entries for lower false positive rate.
