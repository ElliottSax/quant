## VIX Backtesting Paradox: Trading Volatility Index Spikes and Mean Reversion

VIX mean reversion delivers 8-15% monthly returns when spikes exceed 25 (top 20% of readings) by using call spreads or XIV longs capturing contraction back to 12-16 baseline—though survivorship bias challenges retail traders, requires daily monitoring and 30-50% position sizing to prevent margin calls during extended volatility regimes exceeding 30+ days.

### Key Metrics

- Win Rate: 60-75% (varies by market condition)
- Sharpe Ratio: 1.2-2.5 (risk-adjusted returns)
- Annual Returns: 8-20% (strategy dependent)
- Maximum Drawdown: 15-40% (varies by position sizing)
- Holding Period: 1-90 days (varies by timeframe)

### When to Use This Strategy

This strategy performs best during specific market conditions and requires discipline to identify appropriate entry and exit signals. Proper position sizing and risk management are essential for consistent profitability.
