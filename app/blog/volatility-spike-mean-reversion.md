## Volatility Spike Mean Reversion

**Volatility spike mean reversion** enters long when VIX exceeds 25 (panic selling creates opportunity), targeting 30-60% gains over 2-8 weeks as fear subsides. Win rates: 70-80% with 5-15% max drawdown; perfect for buy-the-dip strategies. Requires conviction during fear peaks and discipline to exit before VIX rolls back down to 12-15 range.
