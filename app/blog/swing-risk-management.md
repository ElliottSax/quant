## Swing Trade Risk Management Framework

Fixed 2% risk per trade ($1,000 on $50K), dynamic stops at key levels (2ATR), scale out 50/25/25% to protect profits—prevents drawdowns >10% = protects $5K capital while earning $1,500-2,500/month
