## Short Selling Fundamentals

**Short selling for fundamental decay** targets companies with deteriorating earnings, margins, or cash flow; captures 30-50% downside over 3-12 months. Requires deep research (low false positive rates), tight stops (8-15% loss limits), and covering at support. 40-60% win rates with proper thesis validation.
