## Fibonacci Retracement Trading

**Fibonacci retracement trading** uses natural market bounce points (23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%) to identify support and entries in uptrends. Strategy achieves 45-65% annual returns with 12-20% max drawdown, with 2-6 week holding periods targeting resistance levels.
