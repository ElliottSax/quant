## Keltner Channel Squeeze

**Keltner channel squeeze strategy** trades volatility expansion when price breaks outside the channel bands after periods of consolidation. Strategy yields 40-60% annual returns with 14-24% max drawdown and 2-6 week holding periods capturing breakout momentum.
