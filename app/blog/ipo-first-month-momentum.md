## IPO First Month Momentum

**IPO momentum strategy** buys quality IPOs in first week when euphoria peaks, rides momentum 2-6 weeks capturing 20-50% gains before consolidation. Win rate: 55-65%; losses limited to 15-25% via stop orders. Requires avoiding "hot IPO" traps (unprofitable growth, insider lockup expirations). $10,000-15,000 average wins.
