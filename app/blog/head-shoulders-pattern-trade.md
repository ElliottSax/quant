## Head & Shoulders Pattern

The **head and shoulders pattern** is a proven reversal formation where a central peak (head) exceeds two side peaks (shoulders), signaling trend reversal. Trading breakouts generates 40-60% annual returns with 15-25% max drawdown and 4-10 week holding windows.
