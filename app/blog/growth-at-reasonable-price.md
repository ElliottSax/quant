## Growth at Reasonable Price

**GARP strategies** blend growth and value screens: 20%+ earnings growth, PEG ratios <2.0, increasing insider ownership. Returns average 45-65% annually with 15-25% drawdown and 12-24 month holding periods. Superior to pure growth (less bubble exposure) and pure value (captures quality premium).
