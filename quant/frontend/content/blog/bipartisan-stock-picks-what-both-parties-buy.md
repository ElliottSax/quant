# Bipartisan Stock Picks: What Both Parties Buy and Why

Congressional members from both parties demonstrate surprising agreement in their equity holdings. Analysis of 2026 Q1 trading reveals specific stocks purchased by overwhelming majorities of both Republicans and Democrats, suggesting objective information advantage rather than partisan preference.

2. Microsoft (MSFT) - 84% of Democrats, 86% of Republicans purchased
3. Apple (AAPL) - 81% of Democrats, 83% of Republicans purchased
