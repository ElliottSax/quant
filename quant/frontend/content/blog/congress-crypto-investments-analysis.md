## Crypto Holdings Controversy

Notably, STOCK Act filing requirements for cryptocurrency remain unclear:

**Disclosure Ambiguity**:
- Cryptocurrency not explicitly covered by original STOCK Act
- Some argue crypto holdings as "alternative assets" don't require disclosure
- Some argue crypto are "commodities" outside STOCK Act scope
- Result: Many congressional members hold crypto positions without disclosure

This ambiguity allows cryptocurrency holdings to remain substantially hidden from public scrutiny.
