## Leverage Calculation

Options provide leverage through smaller capital requirements:

**Typical Call Option Leverage Example**:
- Stock purchase: $50,000 capital for 1,000 shares at $50/share
- Call option purchase: $2,000 capital for 10 call contracts (100 shares per contract) at $0.20 premium
- Leverage multiple: 25:1 ($50,000 equity exposure with $2,000 capital)

Congressional members achieve 25-40:1 leverage on core positions through options strategies.
