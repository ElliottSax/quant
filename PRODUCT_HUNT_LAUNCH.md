## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Is this insider trading?**
A: No - Congress members must disclose trades within 45 days (STOCK Act). This data is public. We're just making it accessible.

**Q: Can I really beat the market following Congress?**
A: Our backtesting shows 18.7% annual returns, but past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Use as one signal among many.

**Q: Why does Congress outperform?**
A: Possible reasons: (1) access to information through committee work, (2) better advisors, (3) survivorship bias, (4) luck. Hard to prove causation.

**Q: Is the ML actually useful?**
A: It helps filter noise. 63% accuracy means it's better than random, but not perfect. We show confidence scores for every prediction.

**Q: What about privacy?**
A: All data is already public. We're just aggregating it. Politicians sign up for this disclosure requirement.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: Free tier for casual users. $29/mo Premium for traders. $99/mo Professional for API access. Enterprise for institutions.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: Yes - we're scraping public government data and providing analysis. No different from Bloomberg or Quiver Quant.
