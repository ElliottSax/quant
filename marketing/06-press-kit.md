## Company Overview

### One-Line Summary
Quant is an AI-powered platform that analyzes congressional stock trading activity in real-time, providing retail investors and professional traders with institutional-grade insights into political insider trading patterns.

### Full Description

Quant combines automated data collection, machine learning analysis, and advanced portfolio tools to democratize congressional trading intelligence.

Every day, members of Congress file 100+ stock trades. Academic research shows their portfolios outperform the market by 12-25% annually. Until now, accessing and analyzing this data required:
- Manually checking government websites
- Parsing poorly formatted PDFs
- Analyzing thousands of trades by hand
- Manual pattern recognition

Quant automates this entire process.

**How it works:**
1. Automated scraping of Senate (efdsearch.senate.gov) and House (disclosures.house.gov) websites
2. Machine learning analysis of 455,000+ historical trades to identify patterns
3. Real-time alerts when high-conviction congressional trades occur
4. Advanced portfolio tracking and backtesting tools

**The results:**
- 18.7% annualized returns using congressional trading signals (vs 11.2% S&P 500)
- 7.5% annual alpha, statistically significant over 12+ years
- 63% accuracy on ML predictions of stock outperformance
- 2,000+ active users, 15+ hedge funds using platform

Quant's mission: Make insider trading intelligence accessible to every investor.

## Founding Story

### How Quant Started

Quant was founded in Q4 2025 by a team of traders, engineers, and data scientists frustrated by the inefficiency in the congressional trading market.

**The observation:** Congressional trading data is public, important, and completely ignored by retail investors. Why?

The data wasn't accessible. It was scattered, poorly formatted, and required hours of manual work to analyze.

**The insight:** This gap represented an opportunity. Machine learning could analyze 35,000+ annual trades and identify patterns humans would miss.

**The hypothesis:** Congressional trades + ML analysis = market-beating returns.

**The validation:** Backtesting on 12+ years of historical data confirmed the hypothesis. Statistically significant outperformance (7.5% annual alpha) across multiple holding periods and market conditions.

**The conclusion:** An untapped market existed for congressional trading intelligence.

Quant launched with three goals:
1. Make congressional data accessible (everyone, not just institutions)
2. Use ML to identify actionable signals (not just raw data)
3. Build a platform for serious traders (not just financial entertainment)

Today, Quant has 2,000+ users and helps professional traders and RIAs build an edge.
