## General Partner Outreach Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Personalization:** Mention specific content, research, or product from the potential partner
2. **Value first:** Lead with value to them, not what they can do for you
3. **Specificity:** Be specific about what partnership would look like
4. **Options:** Give choices (integration, data licensing, affiliate, etc.)
5. **Timeline:** Suggest clear next steps and timing
6. **Low barrier:** Make it easy to say yes (free trial, demo, API key)
7. **Respect:** Be respectful of their time; offer call only if genuinely exploring partnership

### Follow-Up Strategy

**Email 1:** Initial outreach (above templates)
- Wait 5-7 days for response
- If no response, don't follow up immediately

**Email 2:** Soft follow-up (1-2 weeks later)
- "Wanted to check if this is relevant"
- Include one additional data point or asset
- Propose specific 20-min call

**Email 3:** Final follow-up (1 week later)
- "Last time reaching out"
- Make it easy to respond (yes/no questions)
- Alternative contact if appropriate person is different

**Then:** Move on. Not a good fit if not responding.

### Key Assets to Attach

- One-page overview of Quant
- Sample data (CSV or API response)
- Backtesting results/whitepaper
- API documentation (if technical partner)
- Case study (if relevant)
- Press coverage (if available)

### Email Optimization

**Subject line formula:** [Opportunity/Partnership] + [Their company/interest] + [Specific value]

Examples:
- "Integration Opportunity: Congressional Data for [Platform Name]"
- "Research Partnership: Congressional Trading Dataset"
- "Revenue Share: Congressional Data API"

**Open with:** Specific reference to their work/company (shows you researched)

**Propose:** Specific partnership option (not vague "let's work together")

**Close with:** Clear next step + easy yes (call? Demo? API key?)

## Final Tips

1. **Timing:** Send outreach week 1 after launch (momentum)
2. **Batch:** Send 5-10 partnership emails per week (sustainable)
3. **Personalize:** Each email different, not template-obvious
4. **Follow up:** Most partnerships come from follow-up, not first email
5. **Be genuine:** Only partner where there's real mutual value
6. **Track:** Keep CRM of all outreach and responses
7. **Learn:** Adjust approach based on response rates and conversions
