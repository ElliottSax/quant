## Segmentation Rules

**If user opens all 5 emails, clicks 3+ links:**
- Segment: "Highly engaged"
- Action: Send advanced emails (API tutorials, backtest walkthroughs)
- Offer: Phone demo with team

**If user opens 1-2 emails, no clicks:**
- Segment: "Low engagement"
- Action: Stop sequence after Email 3
- Offer: Re-engage email in 2 weeks with different angle (e.g., "Still not sure? Here's what you're missing")

**If user upgrades to Premium during sequence:**
- Segment: "Converted"
- Action: Switch to onboarding sequence
- Focus: Activation, feature education, API docs

---

## Performance Targets

- **Open rate:** 40-50% (finance emails typically 35-45%)
- **Click rate:** 8-12% (good CTAs)
- **Conversion rate:** 5-10% (Premium upgrades)
- **Unsubscribe rate:** <1% (low)

---

## A/B Testing Suggestions

**Subject lines to test:**
1. "Congress members beat the market" vs "Political trading data"
2. Direct benefit angle vs curiosity angle
3. Numbers-based (18.7% returns) vs pattern-based (coordination signals)

**Content to test:**
1. Length (short vs detailed)
2. Tone (data-driven vs story-driven)
3. CTAs (Upgrade vs Demo vs Learn More)

---

## Follow-Up Sequences

**For non-openers:**
- Resend Email 1 with different subject after 3 days
- Drop Email 3 (social proof) after another 3 days

**For low-engagement in Free tier (30+ days):**
- Send "Missing out?" email highlighting Premium features
- Offer limited-time discount (10% off annual)
- Include usage stats ("Only using 5% of available data!")
